About Lions Community Park

Lion's Community Park is located at 21960 County Road 5. The park offers 4 outdoor shelters, a garage, and a cabin all free to use and is about 50 acres with 1.22 miles of walking trails.


Lions Park Park Amenities Include:

  • Playground
  • Sports Fields
  • Walking Trails
  • Canoe Access
  • (4) Outdoor Shelters
  • Garage available to use
  • Town Hall available for rent
  • Cabin available for use
