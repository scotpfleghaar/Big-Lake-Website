Community Interest

Many residents find living in the country atmosphere of Big Lake inviting even though they may find themselves commuting into the cities to work. This growing community has an industrial base to balance the residential growth. You’ll find affordable start-up housing as well as move-up homes in the Big Lake community. Parks and recreation also plays a vital role in making Big Lake Township a desirable place to live, work and play.
