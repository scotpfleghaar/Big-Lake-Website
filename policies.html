Obtaining a Burning Permit

1 or 3 day Burning Permits can be obtained from the Clerk’s Office during regular business hours for no fee OR you may obtain an Annual Burning Permit for $5 from the DNR Website. To see more info, click on the link to the right. Please remember that spring restrictions and possible burning bans may be in place. All residents are urged to do the majority of their burning when we have snow cover.
